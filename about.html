My Journey into AI

My fascination with artificial intelligence began during my undergraduate studies in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Kallam Haranadhareddy Institute of Technology. What started as curiosity about neural networks evolved into a deep passion for building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

During my final year, I dove deep into Natural Language Processing and completed my first major project: an AI-powered recruitment system that improved candidate-job matching by 40%. This experience showed me the transformative power of AI and set me on the path to becoming a data scientist.

After graduation, I joined Grid Dynamics as a Junior Data Scientist, where I've had the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects involving multi-agent systems, RAG pipelines, and Model Context Protocol servers. Each project has been a learning experience, pushing me to stay at the forefront of AI research and development.
